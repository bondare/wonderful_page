Cifas

Website Design

Cifas are leaders in fraud prevention. They make the UK a safer place to do business, by enabling organisations in every sector to prevent fraud and protect the public. This is achieved by sharing data, spreading knowledge and pushing the capabilities of technology.

www.cifas.org.uk

The Challenge

Cifas approached Wonderful in order to help advise, design and develop a business first innovation project for the organisation in the form of an online interactive annual report.

Cifas wanted to lead the way in their sector by providing an online report where the report could be enhanced by making it much more engaging, user friendly and resonate with readers.

The Cifas team had an in-house videographer so that they could film key executives and staff in order to deliver a much more personalised feel for the viewer. Combined with new editorial, inspiring infographics that contained critical data and results and behind the scenes video content, the plan was to WOW both their readers and organisation challenging the way reporting was informed to the market.

The Solution

Wonderful worked with Cifas to plan this project and discuss all possibilities. We provided an innovative design that met the brand guidelines but added a new and clean professional tone to the entire report which was designed to encourage the engagement.

By breaking content down into sections and sub-pages we aimed to make the annual report more user friendly. By working closely with the client we were able to help and advise on video content offering suggestions and delivering beyond expectations.

A clever easy-to-navigate system was designed so that users could easily go backward or forwards to key areas so that the report delivered a top class user experience. The report contained a lot of information and video and it was key to keep readers interested and not to click off of the report.

The innovation and thinking behind the design was then enhanced during the development stage by adding animations and fades to enhance the user experience. All the content uploaded and pushed live into the Cifas server.

This was first for Cifas and we wanted our report to stand out and lead the way with its digital proposition to encourage our audiences to engage at a deeper level with our results and company information.
