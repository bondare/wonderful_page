Cereal Killer Café

Website Design, Corporate Branding

The Cereal Killer Café is the UK’s first speciality cereal café. The brand is literally obsessed with everything cereal. It sells over 120 different types of cereal from around the world; if they can source it, they will sell it. Whether it is breakfast, lunch or dinner, the nostalgic café atmosphere is electric containing hundreds of pieces of memorabilia from the 80s and 90s.

www.cerealkillercafe.co.uk

The Objective

The two founders gained huge PR around the innovative concept and outgrew their original website. The Cereal Killer Café required a website that really captured the actual ‘in-store’ experience in order to best showcase the amazing experience of the café online.

151,000 Pageviews

Only 3 weeks after the website went live.

41,000 Unique Visitors

Hitting the site after 3 weeks.

Immersive Web Experience

Capturing the feel of the café for website visitors.

The Change

Wonderful organised a photo-shoot in order to build the website around actual visuals of the café. The only authentic way to really showcase the café without being in it was to recreate every aspect with a photograph and join them all up to create each section of the web.

“Working with Wonderful Creative Agency to create our website was a pretty smooth operation. They shared our vision for the website and helped us realise it and we couldn’t be more pleased with the results. The design process was painless, with great communication every step. Our site traffic has increased and bounce rate decreased massively, so it’s been a great experience with an amazing result.”

Garry & Alan Keery, Owners
