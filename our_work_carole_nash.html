Carole Nash

Website Design

Carole Nash is one of the UK’s leading motorcycle insurers with a high national profile and originally approached Wonderful after it became clear that in order to maintain its position within such a competitive marketplace it needed to update its online presence and rethink the online customer journey. Following the launch of this site, Wonderful has become Carole Nash’s digital agency.

www.carolenash.com

The Objective

The Carole Nash website needed updating with better technology, an enhanced user journey and experience in line with the company’s stance as a market leader.

The Change

Little was to change in terms of content, but the 900-page website was to be redesigned, rebuilt and tested within a nine week time frame. In addition, we worked with our SEO team to ensure the new site would remain at the top of search engines, despite the redesign and build.

With Wonderful experts at the helm we implemented robust account management processes with regular communication and delivery updates to the client to ensure a smooth execution and appropriate management of client expectations.

The result? An engaging website that repositions Carole Nash as the leading motorcycle insurer, all within a matter of weeks.

“I couldn’t recommend Wonderful highly enough. They really listened to our ideas and put a lot of thought into how they could be implemented. Deadlines were tight but all surprisingly met, from concept, design through to implementation, it has been a smooth transition and we are very happy with our end result!”

Angela Todd, Online Manager
