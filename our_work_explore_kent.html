Explore Kent

Mobile Apps

Following a competitive tender, Wonderful was commissioned by Explore Kent, to strategise, design and produce a mobile app for IOS and Android – the first of its kind – that would bring together information all of the public parks that come under Explore Kent’s remit in one central, easily accessible location. The app is produced by Explore, a Kent County Council initiative funded by the European INTERREG IV program.

The Objective

Wonderful was to create a hybrid mobile app to show case all of Kent’s 212 different parks. Built for both Android and iOS, this was to be a very data-rich project which required the construction of a complex database and bespoke CMS to ensure the clients’ needs were met.

The Change

This innovative multi-platform mobile app brings data together from 212 sources to create a central mobile application that allows users to search their closest park with the right facilities and activity allowances. The search functionality, ability to segment push notifications for each park, options to download audio guides, view event information, amenities and images make this dynamic app one of a kind. The app delivers complex data requirements and is the first of its kind in the UK to collate this data into a mobile app.

“Wonderful understood the concept of our app project and our goals from the beginning. Being local we have been able to work closely with them to fine tune the app to meet our requirements and it’s been a wonderfully productive process.”

Project Officer

Kent County Council
